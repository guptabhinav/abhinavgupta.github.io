About Me

I am Abhinav Gupta, a post-doctoral research associate in Prof. Wonpil Im's lab at Lehigh University. I earned my doctorate from the Indian Institute of Technology in Kanpur, India. My expertise lies in enhanced sampling methods for free energy calculations. I developed a method called Parallel Bias Temperature Accelerated Sliced Sampling (PBTASS).

Research

My research focuses on molecular dynamics simulations and enhanced sampling techniques to explore free energy landscapes. I aim to develop new methods and apply them to understand complex biological systems better.
